Game Day Grill has been serving good times since 2014.

Our founder, Bart Parram-Albertson, created Game Day Grill with three points in mind.

  • Friendly service.
  • A delicious menu.
  • Entertainment.

Game Day Grill opened its first location in Toledo, Ohio, in February 2014. The chain has since grown to over 20 locations across Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan. We have always committed to be courteous.
